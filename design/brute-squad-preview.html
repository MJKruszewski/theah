Brute Squad

Théah — 7th Sea 2e · GM sheet

A Brute Squad has one statistic — Strength (the number of thugs in it). Heroes spend Raises against it and each Raise cuts Strength by 1; on the Squad's turn it deals Wounds equal to its current Strength. So the Muster below is health and threat in one. Engaging the Squad

How a Brute Squad fights Core p.191–192
  1. Step 1 · Approach
    The Heroes announce their response — attack, run, hide, whatever they like.
  2. Step 2 · Gather Dice
    You name each Hero's Trait + Skill for their Approach. The Consequence is always: take Wounds equal to the Squad's Strength.
  3. Step 3 · Roll & Raises
    Players spend Raises against the Squad — each Raise reduces its Strength by 1.
  4. Step 4 · Brutes' Turn
    If it isn't at zero, the Squad deals Wounds equal to its current Strength, all to one Hero.
  5. Step 5 · Continue?
    If Strength remains it presses on (or surrenders — a guy can hope). Loop to Step 1 until the Heroes fall or the Squad is defeated.
Example A Strength 8 Guard Squad. Miranda spends 3 Raises attacking — the Squad drops to Strength 5. On its turn it deals 5 Wounds to her. Next Round she cuts it to 2; now it only deals 2.

Reorganize

Squads come in packs. Between Rounds, Brute Squads may merge or split: a Strength 5 and a Strength 3 Squad can combine into one Strength 8, or a Strength 10 Squad can break into two Strength 5 Squads. Adjust the Muster and let a second sheet carry the other half. Core p.192
